Roller

Established in November 1998 , Roller’s main activity is to supply its clients with quality conveyor systems and quality conveyor components such as rollers, troughing sets, pulleys, belt cleaners, covers, motorized pulleys, conveyor belts , vibrating equipment (screens, feeders etc.)

Since 2000 Roller becomes official sales agent for Macedonia for the company Rulmeca SpA, which presents the biggest manufacturer of components for conveying systems in the world. With more than 1200 employees working in over 20 production capacities as well as distribution network all over the world, Rulmeca is present in more than 85 countries for its customers.

Today Roller presents an engineering company that obtains construction turn-key projects referred to bulk conveying systems. For these projects we carry out planning , delivery “free to site” including the ready-for-operation assembly.

Why should you use our products?

Because they provide optimal working benefits such as :

  • Maximum lifespan (working hours) of the products
  • Minimum equipment maintenance cost
  • Maximum reliability and constant product quality
